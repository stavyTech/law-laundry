    From humble beginnings to creating unforgettable culinary experiences

    Who We Are

    NanizTreate is a premium catering and event planning company that began as a small home-based business in 2010. Founded by Nani and Zainab, two passionate culinary enthusiasts, we've grown into one of Lagos' most trusted names in small chops and event coordination.

    Our journey started with a simple mission: to bring people together through exceptional food and memorable experiences. Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of clients annually, from intimate family gatherings to large corporate events.

    Our Mission

    To create unforgettable culinary experiences through passion, creativity, and attention to detail.

    Our Vision

    To be Nigeria's premier catering and event planning brand known for excellence and innovation.

    Our Journey

    Milestones that shaped NanizTreate

    2010: Humble Beginnings

    Founded

    Started as a home-based small chops business serving friends and family in Lagos.

    2013: First Commercial Kitchen

    Expansion

    Opened our first commercial kitchen in Victoria Island to meet growing demand.

    2016: Event Planning Services

    Diversification

    Expanded into full event planning services to complement our catering expertise.

    2019: Award Recognition

    Achievement

    Received "Best Catering Service in Lagos" at the Nigerian Hospitality Awards.

    2023: Current Operations

    Today

    Now serving over 500 events annually with a team of 35 dedicated professionals.
